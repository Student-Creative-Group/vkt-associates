Risk Management & Insurance

The practice of identifying and analyzing loss exposures and taking steps to minimize the financial impact of the risks they impose. Verlin discusses personal and business risks with clients and uses insurance products to transfer risk to 3rd parties. Some common personal and business risks include: 

  • Dispute resolution
  • Loss of income
  • Loss of primary residence
  • Job loss
  • Loss to personal and business property
  • Business income loss due to business interruption
  • Loss due to negligence. (general liability)
