About

Over the past 24 years, Verlin has worked in multiple industries honing his skills in banking, government, insurance and information technology. Some of the companies he worked for included ACS, Bank of America, Citigroup, ING and others. He obtained industry certifications with IAUA, IRS, NACVA and the Texas Department of Insurance.

Today, Verlin has primarily one purpose, to help individuals and business owners, manage, protect and grow their cash flow. This is accomplished by scheduling fact finding sessions and working directly with individuals to implement cash flow plans. The goal is to create a collaborative partnership between Verlin and the client.
