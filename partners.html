Partners

 VKT Associates uses business partners to provide a full array of financial services. The company has relationships with the following professionals:

  • Alternate Dispute Resolution - professionals who provide mediation services between parties, resolve issues and can prevent litigation.
  • Attorneys - draft legal documents for service oriented businesses and handle personal and business disputes.
  • Contractors - residential and commercial contractors.
  • Investment Advisors - licensed security professionals who’s primarily focus is to invest and manage their client’s portfolio. 
  • Mortgage professionals - residential and commercial mortgage brokers.
  • Public Adjusters (PA) - individuals who represent the insured when an insurance claim is filed.
